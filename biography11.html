Biography

I traveled a very atypical path, both personally and professionally. I was born in a small village in Homabay Kenya,back then known as Asego kanyanda Kenya ,and raised in 3 different counties,whent to school like any other kind. I speak English fluently,swahili and the international language(dholuo). I have been good at math and science since my childhood, studying and obtaining a bachelor of Degree of Technology in Information and Communication Technology .After and still working in that field for a couple of months, I decided to learn design on my own and have been working as a product designer and front end developer since July, 2016.One of the greatest strenghs about being a designer with an Information & Technology background is that not only I can handle the graphical aspects of a life, but I can also fully understand, participate and even lead the technical aspects of those same life.The easiest thing you can do for your professional career is to help yourself by helping others. As a bonus, it also makes you happier and more productive.
